# Industry Theme: Ferretería Digital Experience

The Ferretería theme lives in `themes/ferreteria`.

## Theme Responsibilities

- Business domain: hardware store / ferretería.
- Commercial narrative for a client currently using an unofficial Mónica workflow.
- Fictional demo data.
- Visual identity over the shared PADBS framework.
- Vocabulary for inventory, quotations, frequent customers, customer credit, suppliers, dispatch, branches, cash register, high-rotation products, and construction materials.
- PADBS 7-Day Launch positioning.

## Implemented Pages

- `index.html`: executive panorama.
- `inventory.html`: inventory, turnover, branch coverage, critical items.
- `quotes.html`: quote-to-sale commercial journey.
- `credit.html`: frequent customers and customer credit visibility.
- `suppliers.html`: supplier and purchase planning.
- `dispatch.html`: branch, counter, warehouse, and delivery coordination.
- `launch.html`: PADBS 7-Day Launch roadmap, ROI questions, and CTA.

## Fictional Data Policy

All customers, suppliers, values, branch names, product quantities, and operational events are fictional. The demo is designed for sales conversation and should not be presented as a live operational system.

## Commercial Positioning

The theme intentionally avoids raw CRUD, ERP-style menus, and low-quality forms. It shows the owner what centralized operational control could feel like before any heavy implementation decision is made.
